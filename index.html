Who I Am

I built my own website.
Then everyone started asking me how.

I'm Nora — a holistic wellbeing practitioner based in Ladywell, SE London. Through my training courses and community, I kept meeting the same women: talented, trained, building something real on Instagram — but with no website to show for it.

The reason was almost always the same. Squarespace costs £20 a month. Wix costs more. When you're already paying for Fresha, a domain, and everything else that goes into running your own practice, that's a monthly fee that just doesn't make sense.

So I started building sites for them. Using the same tools I used for my own — clean, beautiful, hosted for free — with no ongoing cost to them, ever.

Now I offer that to everyone. Whether you're a facialist, a massage therapist, a yoga teacher, or someone launching their first side hustle — if you've been putting off your website because it felt too expensive, too technical, or too overwhelming, I'm here to make it simple.
